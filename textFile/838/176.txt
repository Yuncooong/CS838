Hae Ha Heng Thai Bistro
Menu has all the usual Thai offerings like the noodles (Pad Thai, Drunken Noodles, etc), fried rice dishes, curries, tom yum soup and more. Prices are great at about $10 per entree. Lunch specials are a sweet deal, where you get your choice of entree, fried gyoza, salad and drink (Thai iced tea, etc) for $9. The entrees can be made with your choice of any protein (chicken, tofu, beef, etc). HH is until 7PM with sangria at $5 and other various cocktails (soju cocktails, soju smoothies, etc), red/white wine and beer priced down.

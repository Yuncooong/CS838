{Lola’s} in West Los Angeles is a popular hangout featuring eclectic cuisine that combines classic comfort food with the glitter and glam of new Hollywood. The bar is famous for creating the [Apple Martini], and along with that signature cocktail, the bar serves up well over 50 other custom Martinis. That is right, over 50! Brunch on the weekends is immensely popular as well, and it is no wonder, with [10 flavors of] mimosas and 7 unique bloody marys. Nevertheless, enough about the booze, the food at {Lola’s} is just as popular as the drinks. Dishes like the Baked Brie in Pastry, Lobster Ravioli, Mac and Cheese, and Empanadas keep customers coming back for more.

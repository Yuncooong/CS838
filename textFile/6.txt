Howlin’ Ray’s
Which came first, the hyperbole about the chicken or the egg-asperating hour-long lines? Not sure, but plan to wait in line for about ***one-and-a-half hours***  for some of the best chicken of your life. And it's not just me: transplanted Southerners a-plenty were waiting in line with me and you know they wouldn't do that for nothing. They assured me that IT IS WORTH IT and THEY WERE RIGHT.

{Milo and Olive} offers diners in the Santa Monica area well made comfort food, crafted using the freshest ingredients. This neighborhood bakery and pizzeria serves fresh baked pastries and bread, wood fired pizzas, fresh salads, and small plates to the hungry in search of an intimate dining experience. The communal tables offer diners a chance to meet and greet one another, [while counter] seating allows guests to watch as their meal is created right before their eyes. All pizzas are made with the house dough, and guests can expect eight to ten different pizzas on [a daily basis], depending on the availability of ingredients. The wine and beer list focuses on small production suppliers, and as with all items available at {Milo and Olive}, change on a regular basis. For those looking for fresh baked breads, pastries, and pizzas, visit {Milo and Olive}.
